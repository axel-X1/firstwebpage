Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You would need your Hz student card and some number of credits inside it(money).

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

You would once again need your Hz student card, a document and an email you will send the project to.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Transfer credits from a debit card using the provided Hz ATM, located near enterance on 1st floor.

How can I book a project space?

Using the Hz app, look up which rooms are free for the day, so noone rushes you out.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Make sure no lecturer is using it and reserve it through the selfservice portal.
